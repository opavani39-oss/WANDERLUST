Country: UAE

Dubai is the most populous city in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) and a global business, tourism, and transportation hub known for its skyscrapers, luxury shopping, and rapid modernization. Located on the Persian Gulf coast, it has transformed from a small fishing village into a cosmopolitan metropolis, featuring landmarks like the Burj Khalifa.
