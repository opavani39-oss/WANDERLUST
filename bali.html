Bali is a premier Indonesian province and island, known as the "Island of Gods," famed for its Hindu culture, volcanic mountains, beaches, and rice terraces. Located near Java, it offers a mix of luxury resorts, bustling tourist hubs like Seminyak, and quiet spiritual retreats. Key, top-visited attractions include Uluwatu Temple, Ubud's, and pristine beaches in Nusa Dua.

Average Cost: $1500 - $2500
