Country:maldives

The Maldives is a tropical nation in the Indian Ocean known for its 1,200 coral islands, turquoise lagoons, and luxury overwater resorts. As the world’s lowest-lying country, it offers incredible diving and snorkeling, with a tourism-focused economy and a population of over 530,000, primarily residing in the capital, Malé

Average Cost: $1500 - $2500
